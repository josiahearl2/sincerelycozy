How Swaddling Benefits Your Baby

Swaddling helps recreate a safe, womb-like feeling that can calm babies and improve sleep quality.

It reduces startle reflex wakeups, supports safe back-sleep positioning, and can help families build healthier sleep routines.

Sincerely Cozy swaddles use 95% rayon from bamboo and 5% spandex for breathable comfort, stretch, and everyday durability.
