How to Transition Your Baby From Swaddling

As babies begin rolling, it is time to transition from full swaddling for sleep safety.

Try a one-arm-out method first, then both arms out over 1 to 4 weeks based on your baby’s comfort and readiness.

Your Sincerely Cozy blanket still stays useful as a nursing cover, tummy-time blanket, stroller canopy, and changing blanket.
